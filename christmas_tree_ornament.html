Christmas Tree Ornament

The Smart Gift That Blinks

Introduction

This is a project which is being developed for christmas gifts in the 2026 season. I am creating this mostly as an exercise in driving LEDs, microcontroller implimentation, and experimenting with solar cells in small, portable applications.

Theory of Operation

Physical Description

A bare PCB cut to the shape of a tree, circular ornament or other holiday themed shape. The front of the device will feature an LED matrix to show animations. The back will feature the microcontroller circuitry, the solar cells and power regulation and energy storage. The top of the PCB will have a hole for hanging. The constraints on size and weight will be dictated by the weight limits of a typical christmas tree branch, and having a surface area that fits in with other ornaments.

Functional Description

The device will run on a LFP cell which is charged by a series of solar cells. Determining the on-time/ off-time cycle of the device will need to be determined after an analysis of the available light energy in the typical living room during the winter.
